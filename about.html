About The Father's House, All Nations Church (TFH ANC)

The Father's House Inc. (TFH) is an IPA-registered entity (5-109620) registered on the 31st March 2021. TFH represents a network of churches across Papua New Guinea (PNG) that are in direct relationship established in covenant with Pastor Ben Minok.

Why it is called The Father's House? Jesus himself while in his earthly ministry was the Father's House. The Father, the Holy Spirit, the fullness of the Godhead came and dwelt with him in bodily form. Today, the church as a corporate body is the exact replacement of Jesus on earth. The church is now The Father's House on earth.

All Nations Church (ANC) is the name of the Port Moresby base also under Senior Pastor Ben Minok. ANC is a full gospel, apostolic and prophetic kingdom church based in the capital city of PNG.

As of 9th April 2021, TFH ANC under Pastor Ben Minok is also under the direct spiritual input and covering of Dr. Jonathan David., the presiding apostle of the International Strategic Alliance of Apostolic Churches (ISAAC) Network.

As of 20th April 2021, Pastor Ben Minok and his wife Jessica are registered into the ISAAC Network as 1st Generation ISAAC Pastors.

Consequently, TFH ANC is the newest ISAAC Church in PNG (est. 2021).

About Pastor Ben Kandaki Minok

Pastor Ben Kandaki Minok is the Founder and Senior Elder of The Father’s House Inc., and also the Senior Pastor of All Nations Church, Port Moresby.

He is the spiritual son of the late Apostle Suckling Tamanabae. He has been trained in Dr. Jonathan David's Permanent School of the Prophets in Muar, Malaysia and returns there annually. He relates to Dr. Jonathan David, as his primary apostolic source.

Saved and baptised at the age of 12 in a Norman Armstrong crusade at CRC Bethel Centre, Waigani, Port Moresby in 1992, he has been involved in ministry under the late Apostle Pastor Suckling Tamanabae for over 20 years starting from his high school days, and later in pastoral leadership with Covenant Ministries International until Pastor Suckling's passing in July 2020. His core focus is on prayer, intercession, and the prophetic.

His passion is to see a generation awakened to making the First Commandment (Mat 22:37) the first place in their lives. To stand before the audience of One by cultivating the secret place of prayer.

He is also a registered pharmacist by profession with certification under the Papua New Guinea Pharmacy Board, and is currently the Pharmacist-Branch Manager at Johnston’s Pharmacies Ltd, Downtown Port Moresby.

Pastor Ben is married to Jessica from the Philippines, and they have one daughter.
